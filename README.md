# AI Medical Research Toolkit

Open-source prompts, templates, and workflows for AI-assisted medical research writing, literature synthesis, case report drafting, and academic workflow design.

## Purpose

This repository shares practical ways to use AI tools in medical research while keeping human review, scientific judgment, and research integrity central.

## Who this is for

- medical researchers
- clinicians
- fellows
- students
- academic writers

## What this repository includes

- prompt templates for literature review and case reports
- workflows for abstracts, posters, and manuscripts
- structured methods for AI-assisted drafting
- practical examples for research writing support

## Important note

This repository is for research and writing support only.
It does not provide clinical advice.
All outputs must be checked against source material, guidelines, and institutional standards.
